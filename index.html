Frequently Asked Questions

What age groups can join?

We offer classes for all age groups starting from 4 years old to seniors. Check our Kids and Senior programs.

Do beginners need prior experience?

No, we welcome beginners with open arms. Our instructors are trained to teach from scratch.

Are trial classes available?

Yes! Book a free trial class to experience our studio and teaching style before committing.

Can wedding choreography be customized?

Absolutely. Every wedding is unique. We tailor choreography to your song selection, family size, and preferences.

Do you offer online sessions?

Yes, we have hybrid plans and online fitness/yoga sessions available for remote learners.
